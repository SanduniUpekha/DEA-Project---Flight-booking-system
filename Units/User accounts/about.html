About Us

We are,

Pheonix Airline PVT was a major Srilankan airline that operated from 1937 until its merger with Srilankan Airlines in 2015. It was originally founded in Katunayake as a mail delivery airline called All Srilankan Aviation, which soon became a commercial passenger airline.
